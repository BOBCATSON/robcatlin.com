Who am I

A Front End Web developer from Hertfordshire, UK.
The day I realised I could bend the web to my will with nothing more than a text editor my life was forever changed. I knew that web development was made for me.

About my work

I love Front End development and the satisfaction of making pixel perfect things for the web. I am always learning and looking for a new tool or framework to improve my workflow. When I take on a new project I like to use the best tool for the job, and if that involves learning something new then that makes me happy.

Education

I am a self-taught developer who loves to learn. I have enrolled on a few courses to increase my knowledge. The web is constantly evolving and growing. A developers reading list is never finished.
