Who am I

Rob Catlin, a Front End web developer from Hertfordshire, UK. I love tech. All types of tech. The day I realised I could bend the web to my will just by using a text editor my life was forever changed. I knew that web development was made for me. People will pay me money to do this? I still ask myself this question and wonder how I do a job I love every day and get paid to do it.

About my work

I love Front End development and the satisfaction of making pixel perfect things for the web. I am always learning, always looking for a new tool or framework to improve my workflow. When I take on a new project I like to use the best tool for the job, and if that involves learning something new then that makes me happy inside.
